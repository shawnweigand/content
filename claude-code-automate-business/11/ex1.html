Phase 01
The Interview
T1
Business Foundation
What does your business do, and what's the team size?
What three things take the most time each week?
What tools does your team use daily?
Listening for: stack, scale, context
T2
Pain Points
What's the most repetitive task that shouldn't need a human?
Where do things fall through the cracks?
Listening for: friction, inconsistency, waste
T3
Current Automation
Do you have automation in place? How well is it working?
What have you tried that didn't stick?
Listening for: gaps, failed attempts, readiness
T4
Goals & Stakes
If one workflow ran itself — which one?
What does success look like in 90 days?
Listening for: vision, constraints, priority
Conversational — 3–4 questions per turn, not a form.
